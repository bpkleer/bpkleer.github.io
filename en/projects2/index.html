From October 2015 to September 2022 I teached at Justus-Liebig-University Giessen at least one course each term. My teaching focused on introductory courses of empirical methods or programs in the BA or MA program at the department of political science.

Besides my regular teaching, I created together with Simone Abendschön a web-based-training that accompany the two statistics lectures in the BA program. This web-based-training includes five modules and spans over the complete topics of both lectures (descriptive and inference statistics). Simone Abendschön and I got also funding (QSL-D) for another project (Statistics+) in which we renew the introductory course on R completely. We integrate recent didactics developments in teaching statistics and programming. In addition we also built a web-based-training for introductory courses in SPSS with funding from HessenHub.
